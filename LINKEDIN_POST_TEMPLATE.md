## Tips for Your LinkedIn Post:

### 1. **Add Visuals**

- Screenshot of your before/after Trello boards
- Code snippet from your script
- Flowchart of the automation process
- Your learning path visualization

### 2. **Best Posting Times**

- Tuesday-Thursday: 9-11 AM
- Wednesday is typically best for engagement

### 3. **Engagement Hooks**

Start with:

- "3 months ago, I was overwhelmed..."
- "I made a mistake that cost me 30 hours..."
- "Here's what I learned building my first automation..."

### 4. **Call-to-Action**

End with:

- "What do you automate in your learning?"
- "Drop a â­ if you found this helpful!"
- "Follow for more DevOps content"

### 5. **Hashtag Strategy**

Use 3-5 main hashtags: #DevOps #Automation #Python
Plus 3-5 niche hashtags: #Kubernetes #Terraform #AWS

### 6. **Tag People/Companies**

- Tag Trello if relevant
- Tag DevOps influencers you follow
- Tag bootcamp/course if applicable
